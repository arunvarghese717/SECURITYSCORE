Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 20, 2025 | Reading time: 6 minutes

MySecurity Scores (“we”, “us”, “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy and personal data. This Privacy Policy details how we manage, use, and safeguard your personal information in compliance with global data protection regulations, including GDPR, as of **2026**.

1. What Data We Collect

We only collect data necessary to provide and improve our security assessment services:

  • **User Identity Data:** Information provided during account registration (e.g., email address, name).
  • **Security Assessment Information:** Domain names, public IP addresses, and non-sensitive configuration details you input for scanning.
  • **Usage Data:** Platform analytics, interaction logs, and device information to monitor service performance and usability.
  • **Cookies:** Data collected via cookies for site functionality and analytics (see section 3).

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is strictly used for the following purposes:

  • To provide accurate and personalized security scoring and assessment reports.
  • To improve and optimize the functionality and reliability of the MySecurity Scores platform.
  • To communicate service updates, security alerts, and account-related information.
  • For internal legal, compliance, and auditing purposes.

3. Cookies and Tracking Policy

We use cookies—small files stored on your device—to enhance your experience:

  • **Strictly Necessary Cookies:** Essential for core site function and account access.
  • **Analytics Cookies:** Used to track user behavior anonymously to improve site layout and content.
  • **Advertising Cookies (AdSense):** Used by third-party services like Google AdSense to serve relevant advertisements. You can manage or disable these cookies through your browser settings or AdSense's consent tools.

4. Data Sharing and Security

We **do not sell** your personal information. Data may be shared only with necessary, vetted third-party service providers (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics tools) under strict confidentiality agreements. We employ advanced technical and organizational measures (encryption, access control) to protect your stored data.

5. Your User Rights (GDPR and CCPA)

As a user, you retain control over your personal data. You have the right to:

  • **Access:** Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • **Rectification:** Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • **Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"):** Request the deletion of your personal data under certain conditions.
  • **Restriction of Processing:** Request that we limit the way we use your data.
  • **Data Portability:** Request that we transfer your data to another organization.

To exercise any of these rights, please use the contact information provided below.

Have Questions about your Data?

We believe in full transparency regarding your data. If you have any concerns or wish to exercise your data rights, please reach out directly.
